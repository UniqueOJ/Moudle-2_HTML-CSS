Idanre Hills_Beautiful Tourist Attraction in Ondo

Idanre Hills also known as Oke Idanre is one of the popular tourist attractions in Ondo.
It is an ancient city set upon A hill located in the city of Idanre Akure, Ondo State, Nigeria.
The site has been accorded topmost by the state government in her tourist development programs since the year 1998 till date.
The hills divide Idanre into two segment namely old settlement at the top of the hills while the new settlement is at the base of the hills,
The Hills are located on hundreds of hectares of land surrounded by beautiful vegetations, high hills, ancient settlements etc.
it is about 3000 feet above sea level.

Climbing the Hills is not a journey for the faint-hearted. The journey requires strength, resilience and stamina.
The hills are awe-inspiring, it offers a beautiful view of Idanre settlement down the hills.
“Oke Idanre” consists of unique peculiarities, ancient wonders and beliefs, various attractions to tourists. This includes;

  1. “Ibi Akaso” The Steps.

    2. Ibi Akaso, meaning "the steps on the hill", it is a significant attraction.
    From top to bottom, it has six hundred eighty-two steps and five resting points.

  2. The 17th Century Kings Palace.

    3. Also known as Owa's Palace, it is an old palace that dates back to the 17th century.
    It was the people’s palace before they moved.

  3. The First School.

    4. The first primary school in Idanre is also located on the hill.
    The school was built in 1896 and it is still in existence till day.
    However, it is now a rusty place with no form of educational activity.

  4. Intriguing “Agboogun” footprints on the rock

    .

  5. Arun River

    6. This river is believed to have supernatural powers to heal various sickness and diseases.
    The Arun river is located on top of the mountain.

  6. The Prison

    7. The court also has a prison yard located opposite the court where prisoners and offenders are kept.

  7. The Courts


    8. The city also has an ancient court of law where local matters are settled.
    The court was built in 1906 by the British missionaries.
    This is to ensure law and order within the community. It has the main court, the judge’s chamber etc.
